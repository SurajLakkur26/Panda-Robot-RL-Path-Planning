RL-Based Path Planning for Dynamic Robot Operation

Master thesis at Fraunhofer IGCV

Simulation-driven SAC path planning in PyBullet for a 7-DOF Franka Panda robot operating in dynamic environments.

Result: Up to 89% task success and approximately 98% safe operation over 10,000 episodes.

Project Details

  • What it does: Learns safe, collision-free paths for a Franka Panda in environments with moving obstacles.
  • How it works: Uses SAC-based reinforcement learning in PyBullet with safety-aware observations (obstacle velocity, line-of-sight, etc.).
  • Result: Evaluated over 10,000 episodes with strong task success and safe operation.
